{block:Homepage}

Alibi is a new sort of magazine focused on literature and rock & roll. It’s published using a mix of traditional and innovative approaches – not only online, not only print, but a unique blend of the page, the stage, and the web. No high-quality magazine exists that presents rock music and literature side-by-side and explores their exhilarating relationship.
Not until now. Keep an eye on this site for new films, audio, and writing, and for news on our debut print magazine and live event, starring Neko Case, launching this winter.
