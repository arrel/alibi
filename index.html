Alibi is a new sort of magazine focused on literature and rock & roll. It’s published using a mix of traditional and innovative approaches – not only online, not only print, but a unique blend of the page, the stage, and the web.  No high-quality magazine exists that presents rock music and literature side-by-side and explores their exhilarating relationship. Not until now. Keep an eye on this site for new films, audio, and writing, and for news on our debut print magazine and live event, starring Neko Case, launching this winter.

First Issue of Alibi is Taking Shape

July 22, 2011

Several terrific authors and musicians have signed on for the first issue of Alibi, due out this winter. The print mag will appear quarterly, and the debut issue will feature new writing by Tobias Wolff, Jim White, Zach Rogue, Kim Addonizio, Blake Hazard, Ted Gioia, Adrian McKinty, Viva Las Vegas, and many others.  The magazine will contain interviews, short stories, essays, and a handful of features in which musicians write about books and authors write about music.

In July, the Alibi crew will be filming a mini-concert with Rogue Wave's frontman. Zach has a new solo record coming out soon under the artist name Release the Sunbird. He's invited us over to his house for an exclusive backyard concert, which we will film and then post here on alibimagazine.com. This will be the first of an exclusive series of mini-concerts and mini-readings that we'll feature on the site.
